INTRODUCTION

Before anything else, I would like to introduce myself. Hello, I am Moises Danielle Tulinao, currently going at the University of Cebu as a BSIT student.

IT THAN BSME

Going back now, why did you choose IT?, Well, to be honest, at first, I didn't plan on becoming an IT student, I choose the STEM strand in hoping to become a bonified Marine Engineering oneday, I was greatly inspired by my Father who is one, travelling the seven seas. I really thought that I was going to become like that, but after I graduated SHS I was faced by my fathe's and mother's disapproval on becoming someone who will one day sail the seas. After many arguments and fights, not only did I lose the war, but I feel like i've lost my long dream. But instead of going to despair, I picked myself up and googled the most demanding job and the most high-paying job in the Philippines. And there I saw it, "IT makes around 15-50k as an intern". Without second thought, I chose IT, not because I was inspired, not because it was my dream, but because I know that I will be rich while working home. That's all for my reason on why I chose IT as my course, but one thing for certain, I will come to love this course, even if it's hard and tiring, as long as I have the determination and set goals, I know that I will succedd in this industry someday.
